Bilaawgii Safarkayaga

Sahal Academy waxa la aasaasay 2020, iyadoo ujeedadu ahayd in la helo xarun casri ah oo lagu horumariyo da'yarta jecel kubadda cagta. Waxaan ku bilownay koox yar, balse maanta waxaan nahay mid ka mid ah xarumaha ugu waaweyn ee soo saara ciyaartoy tayo leh.

  • 2020 - La aasaasay
  • 2021 - Guushii ugu horreysay ee tartan maxalli ah
  • 2022 - Tababaro heer caalami ah
  • 2023 - Ciyaartoygii ugu horreeyay ee dibadda loo xusho

Hadafkeena

Inaan soo saarno ciyaartoy leh xirfad sare, anshax wanaagsan, iyo karti ay ku gaaraan heer caalami ah.

Himiladeena

Inaan noqono akadeemiyada ugu fiican ee soo saarta da'yarta kubadda cagta ee gobolka oo dhan.

Qiimahayaga

Waxaan aaminsanahay shaqo wadareed, dadaal, is-ixtiraam, iyo horumar joogto ah.

Horumarinta hibada da'yarta kubadda cagta ee Somaliland, anagoo dhiseyna anshaxa iyo horumarka shaqsiyeed.
